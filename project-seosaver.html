SEO Saver

SEO Saver is a lightweight, easy-to-use tool designed to help developers, marketers, and content creators optimize their websites for search engines. This project streamlines the process of improving SEO (Search Engine Optimization) performance by analyzing key SEO factors, offering real-time suggestions, and tracking progress over time.

  • Meta Tag Analysis

    Evaluate the quality and effectiveness of your meta tags (titles, descriptions, keywords) to ensure they are optimized for search engines.

  • SEO Health Check

    Perform a quick health check of your website, identifying potential issues such as missing alt attributes, broken links, or incorrect header hierarchy.

  • Keyword Suggestions

    Get actionable keyword suggestions based on your content to improve visibility and ranking.

  • Performance Metrics

    Track and visualize SEO performance through a user-friendly dashboard, helping you monitor improvements or identify areas needing attention.

  • Page Speed Optimization Tips

    Get recommendations for improving page load speeds, ensuring a better user experience and enhanced ranking.
